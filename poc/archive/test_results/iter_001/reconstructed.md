# Test Doc 4 Dress Code

### 2.4. Student Dress Code — General Guidance

- **Headwear:** Hats will only be worn during playground time to protect from the sun.
- **Headwear:** Religious headwear may always be worn, provided it does not obscure the student's vision; it must be removed for all PE lessons for health and safety reasons.
- **Headwear:** Hair can be any length and any style. For PE, long hair must be tied back for safety.
- **Footwear:** Flat, enclosed shoes must be worn outdoors — comfortable, fastened with laces, buckles, zips, or velcro, and students must be able to put them on and take them off independently.
- **Footwear:** Indoor shoes must be closed-toe to ensure safe walking; no bulky, furry, or flip-flop styles. Anti-slip socks are also recommended.
- **General Everyday Style:** Jewellery must not dangle; no hoop earrings or large necklaces (health and safety). On PE days, students must be able to remove stud earrings or other items as required.
- **General Everyday Style:** Subtle make-up may be worn from Year 7 upwards (e.g., mascara, powder, foundation, nail varnish); no lipstick.
- **General Everyday Style:** Acrylic nails may not be longer than two millimetres from the fingertip (health and safety).
- **General Everyday Style:** Shorts and skirts must cover the upper thighs (¾ length or longer). Cycling shorts are permitted on PE days but must also cover the upper thighs.
- **General Everyday Style:** All clothes must be without holes or rips. All underwear must be covered by outerwear.
- **PE Day:** Students must wear appropriate sports underwear, shorts/leggings/tracksuit bottoms, a t-shirt or sweatshirt, and trainers.
As an inclusive school, we ask that our whole community respects these guidelines.

### 2.5. Staff Dress Code

**Acceptable clothing** includes: appropriate-length skirts and dresses (knee-length), blouses, smart t-shirts, polo shirts, trousers, smart jeans, chinos.


- **Not acceptable:** Mini-skirts, hot pants, or lycra cycling shorts shorter than mid-thigh.
- **Not acceptable:** Leisure shorts, tracksuits, or sportswear (except for PE and associated activities).
- **Not acceptable:** Transparent or see-through blouses, dresses, or shirts; low-cut tops; crop-tops; vest/spaghetti-strap tops.
- **Not acceptable:** Clothing with tears, holes, or rips; clothes bearing offensive logos, slogans, or graphics.
- **Not acceptable:** Indoor wearing of baseball caps or hats; high-heeled stiletto shoes or beach flip-flops (indoors).
- **Not acceptable:** Clothes that restrict movement or can snag on equipment (loose pockets, sequins, large buttons).
- **Not acceptable:** Underwear worn in a revealing manner.
